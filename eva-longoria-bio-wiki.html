Eva Longoria Bio (Wiki)

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, September 15, 2024

Who is Eva Longoria?

Eva Longoria is an American actress, producer, director, activist, and businesswoman.

She is immensely popular for her role as Isabella Brana on the CBS daytime soap opera ‘The Young and the Restless’.

Also known for her role as  Gabrielle Solis on the ABC television series ‘Desperate Housewives.

She played in the film ‘Low Rider’ portraying the character of Gloria.

Eva Longoria: Age, Parents, Ethnicity

Eva was born on 15 March 1975, in Corpus Christi, Texas, United States of America.

Her nationality is American and her ethnicity is Mexican-American.

Eva Longoria was born to Ella Eva (Mireles) and Enrique Longoria, Jr. Eva Longoria was raised as a Roman Catholic along with her three sisters.

Longoria was very interested in acting and public speaking and always exhibited her actively participating traits.

Being a member of a medium-class family, she worked in Wendy’s restaurant part-time for three years.

Education

Talking about her educational background Eva Longoria, attended Texas A&M University-Kingsville, where she majored in kinesiology.

She also applied for Chicano Studies at California State University, Northridge.

While she was admitted to Texas A&M University-Kingsville, she participated in Miss Corpus Christi USA 1998 and won the title which is the groundwork for her success in the field of acting.

Eva Longoria: Professional Life, Career

Eva Longoria initiated her career as an actor in ‘Beverly Hills, 90210’ in which she was just a guest star.

In 2001, she got a breakout role as Isabella Brana in the CBS Daytime soap opera ‘The Young and the Restless.

In 2003, she left ‘The Young and the Restless’ and starred in ABC comedy-drama ‘Desperate Housewives’ as Gabrielle Soils which made her eligible to be nominated for a ‘Golden Globe Award for Best Performance by an Actress in a Television Series – Musical or Comedy.

After being appointed as the first face of ‘Bebe Sport’ and holding a contract with ‘L’Oréal’, ‘Hanes’, and ‘New York & Co.’, she hosted ‘MTV Europe Music Awards 2010’.

She peaked at #4 on Forbes Prime Times’s 10 Top-Earning Women with an estimated $12 million.

In 2015 she acted as a ‘telenovela’ actress in a sitcom called ‘Telenovela’.

Apart from her acting career, Eva Longoria is also an established businesswoman having some restaurants including ‘Por Fin’.

Eva Longoria: Salary, Net Worth

As of 2022, she has a net worth of $50 million according to the sources but her salary is not revealed yet.

Eva Longoria: Rumors, Controversy

Eva Longoria is currently not involved in any sort of rumors or controversies because of her marital relationship with Jose Baston.

But, earlier, due to numerous relationships with celebrities, she was often a headline in celebrity news portals.

Height, Weight, Body Size

Eva Longoria has a height of 5 feet 2 inches. Her body weighs 53kg.

She has dark brown hair and black eyes.

Her bra size is 32c, the dress size is 6US and the shoe size is 6US.

Besides these, Eva Longoria’s body measurements are 35-25-35 inches.
