Maino &amp; Wack 100 Get Into Heated Argument Over Troy Ave's Chain

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, July 19, 2024

Maino and Wack 100 have gotten into a heated argument over Troy Ave‘s “fake” chain.

Maino recently called Troy out for allegedly wearing fake jewelry and claimed he was in possession of a counterfeit Jesus piece chain that belonged to him.

Earlier this week, Wack 100 came to Troy’s defense and pulled up old comments from Maino showing love to the Brooklyn rapper following the deadly 2016 shooting at New York’s Irving Plaza that resulted in the death of his bodyguard, Ronald “Banga” McPhatter.

“For the last five years Troy Ave, his team and I have had a great working relationship,” Maino wrote on Instagram at the time. “Not only have we performed and done several songs together but we’ve had many personal conversations about life our goals and our efforts as musicians.

“Despite media reports to the contrary, there are no ‘ongoing beefs’ or ‘entourage issues.’ It always has been and always will be good relations between myself and my team and Troy Ave, Hovain, Young Lito and the BSB crew.”

Wack 100 wrote in the caption: “Dam @mainohustlehard Pretty strong Respectful words at the start after the situation. Seems like if you would of picked up @troyave chain that you obviously had No issues with according to YOUR post.

“The honorable thing to do is return it that night of the following day …. But you held it 7 yrs … Kinda weird but #CloutisTheNewCocain.”

Maino fired back in the comments section, writing: “Mind your fucking business you dick eating f-ggot! Fuck is you speaking on me pussy!!!”

Wack 100 then attempted to get Maino dropped by FOX Soul — which airs his Kitchen Talk show — by screenshotting his comment and tagging the streaming platform in a separate post.

“@foxsoul I hear you run a unbiased respectful network,” he said. “@mainohustlehard doesn’t seem to know this. We respect all #LGBTQIA they didn’t deserve this hate @hollywoodunlocked.”
