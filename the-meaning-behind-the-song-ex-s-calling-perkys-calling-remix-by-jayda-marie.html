The Meaning Behind The Song: Exs Calling Perkys Calling Remix by Jayda Marie

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, August 19, 2024

As a DJ, I often come across many incredible songs that leave a lasting impact on me. One such song is “Ex’s Calling ‘’Perkys Calling Remix’’” by Jayda Marie. I remember stumbling upon this song at a friend’s house, and from the moment I heard it, I was captivated by its raw emotions and relatable lyrics.

The lyrics of “Ex’s Calling ‘’Perkys Calling Remix’’” delve deep into the post-breakup feelings and experiences that many of us can relate to. The song begins with a desperate call from the protagonist to their ex, expressing a desire to work things out and possibly reconcile. However, as the song progresses, it becomes evident that the ex’s constant calling only serves as a reminder of their inability to move on.

The lyrics in the first verse shed light on the protagonist’s frustration towards their ex’s attempts to move on and upgrade their life. They admonish the ex for not sticking to their promises of change and for underestimating their own worth. The protagonist asserts their independence and newfound success, declaring that they have moved on from the past and have no intention of falling back into the same cycle.

In the second verse, the lyrics take a slightly more confrontational tone, with the protagonist confidently asserting their new flame and the luxurious life they are now living. They mock their ex for their foolishness in thinking they could hold them back. The protagonist’s confidence and resilience shine through as they reflect on how their ex’s doubts only fueled their determination to succeed.

The chorus repeats throughout the song, emphasizing the protagonist’s astonishment at the ex’s inability to let go and move on. The repetitive nature of the chorus highlights the frustration and disbelief that the protagonist feels towards their ex’s persistence.

The title of the song, “Ex’s Calling ‘’Perkys Calling Remix’’”, brings attention to the original song that served as the foundation for this remix. Jayda Marie’s remix is a refreshing take on Future’s “Perkys Calling,” adding her unique perspective and showcasing her talent as an artist.

Listening to “Ex’s Calling ‘’Perkys Calling Remix’’” by Jayda Marie, I can’t help but be reminded of my own experiences with past relationships. We’ve all had those exes who just can’t seem to let go, and this song captures those emotions perfectly. It serves as both a relatable anthem for anyone who has experienced the aftermath of a breakup and an empowering reminder of the importance of self-worth and moving forward.

In conclusion, “Ex’s Calling ‘’Perkys Calling Remix’’” is a powerful and relatable song that explores the emotions and experiences surrounding a breakup. Jayda Marie’s remix adds her own flair to the original track, delivering a captivating portrayal of resilience and growth. This song resonates with listeners by conveying the frustration, astonishment, and determination that come with the end of a relationship.
