Gina Torres Wiki Biography

Born on the 25th April 1969, in Manhattan, New York City, Gina Torres is of Cuban descent. She is a film and television actress who has appeared in successful television series such as “Hercules”, “Xena – Warrior Princess”, and most recently “Suits”. She has also landed roles in feature films, including “Matrix Reloaded”, “Matrix Revolutions” and “South of Pico”.

Estimations suggest that Gina Torres’ net worth is as much as $8 million, as of mid-2017. She has been accumulating her wealth from her active involvement in the entertainment industry since 1992.

Gina Torres Net Worth $8 Million

Gina grew up in The Bronx and attended the prestigious High School of Music and Art in New York, where she was trained as a mezzo soprano. Unable to afford college, she took several acting jobs.

In 1992, she landed her first notable television role in the series “Law & Order”, and later appeared again three years later but in a different role. Torres made several appearances in the soap opera “One Life to Live” in 1995 and ’96. She also had guest appearances in various series including “NYPD Blue” (1995), “Dark Angel” (1996), “Profiler” (1997) and “Xena – Warrior Princess” (1997). In 1998, she was cast in the television series “Nikita” as lesbian enemy agent Jenna Vogler. In 2000, she landed her first major role, as Helen Carter in the television series “Cleopatra 2525”, winning an ALMA Award for Outstanding Lead Actress in a Syndicated Drama Series. Her series “Alias” and roles in various TV shows and films have contributed to her net worth.

Gina’s film career includes appearances in “Bed of Roses” in 1996, supporting roles in “The Substance of Fire”, and the Matrix sequels “The Matrix Reloaded” and “The Matrix Revolutions”, all in 2003. She has also starred in films such as “Serenity”, “Five Fingers”, and “I Think I Love My Wife” alongside Chris Rock. Her net worth has been supplemented by recent appearances in “The Catch”, “Claws” and “Final Space”.

In her personal life, Gina has been married to Laurence Fishburne since 2002, and they have a daughter together. However, she announced their separation in late 2017.
