Larry David's oldest daughter, Cazzie David is an actress/writer whose ex-boyfriend was Pete Davidso

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, September 25, 2024

Cazzie David is famous for a number of reasons. She is an actress and writer mostly known as the daughter of Larry David. Cazzie also gained significant attention for her relationship with stand-up comedian, Pete Davidson. She published her first book, a collection of essays titled, "No One Asked for This" in 2020.

Cazzie wrote and starred on the web series, Eighty-Sixed. Further, she has written for the Hollywood Reporter and Vanity Fair as well. Stay with us to know more about her romantic affairs and her personal life.

First Born Daughter of Larry and Laurie David

Cazzie Laurel David was born on May 10, 1994, in California, the United States. She is the first daughter of Larry David and his ex-wife, Laurie David. Her father is widely popular for creating one of the greatest television sitcoms, Seinfeld. Further, he created another hit HBO series, Curb Your Enthusiasm.

Meanwhile, Cazzie's mother Laurie is an environmental activist who previously worked as a talent manager. She produced the 2006 Academy Award-winning documentary, An Inconvenient Truth. Likewise, she and Katie Couric are the executive producers of the 2014 film, Fed Up.

Cazzie has a younger sister named, Romy David, who was born on March 2, 1996. She is an actress who appeared on Hannah Montana along with her sister and her father.

Larry and his ex-wife, Laurie was married from 1993 until 2007. Currently, Larry is married to Ashley Underwood, while Laurie maintains a low-profile.

Cazzie David and Pete Davidson's Relationship

The 26-year-old, Cazzie was in a high-profile relationship with the Saturday Night Live cast member, Pete Davidson. They first met when Cazzie's father hosted the show back in 2015. Afterward, Cazzie and Pete started dating in early 2016.

Everything seemed fine until one day Davidson called off their relationship. Cazzie claimed that he ended their two-year relationship over text in May 2018. The next day she found out the comedian had covered the tattoos dedicated to her and moved on with Ariana Grande.

Further, just two weeks after he split with Cazzie, Davidson became engaged to Grande in June 2018. Seeing her ex-boyfriend, Cazzie told that she went through severe anxiety at the time. However, Pete and Ariana called off their engagement in October.

Remarks about her Ex-Boyfriend

Cazzie never commented publicly about her ex-boyfriend's relationships. She didn't call him a cheater nor she created any scene. However, she mentioned their bitter break-up in her debut book, No One Asked for This.

It seems Pete is cool with his ex-girlfriend. Upon Cazzie's book, he said:

"I'm really happy for her. It did well, and, you know, we're cool. I wish her nothing but the best. I think she wrote a really funny book and I hope it stays on the chart."

Cazzie's first book was released in November 2020, and it debuted as the No. 2 books on The New York Times' paperback nonfiction best-seller list. Recently, Cazzie was linked up with singer and musician, John Mayer.

Cazzie David Net Worth

There is no actual information about Cazzie's net worth. However, she obviously enjoys her father's net worth which is estimated to be nearly a billion dollars. So, Larry along with Jerry earns a vast majority of their incomes from syndications, DVD sales, merchandise, and royalties. Through 2010, Seinfeld made $2.7 billion, while in 2017, the show generated made an estimated $4.06 billion in syndication. Additionally, Netflix bought Seinfeld for $500 million in 2019.
