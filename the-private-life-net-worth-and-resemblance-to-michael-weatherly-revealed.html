The Private Life, Net Worth, and Resemblance to Michael Weatherly Revealed!

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, August 18, 2024

Keeping a private profile, August Manning Weatherly is the eldest son of the actor Michael Weatherly. He chooses to stay away from the public eye and media attention. August is said to bear a strong resemblance to his father, Michael.

Most people know August for being the son of the actor Michael and his ex-wife, Amelia Heinle. But many may not be aware that he is not the only child in the Weatherly family. Explore more about the eldest son of Michael, August, and also learn about his parent's relationship and his siblings.

Who is August Manning Weatherly?

August is the son of an American actor and producer, Michael, and his former wife, Amelia, who also is an actress. Born on January 10, 1996, August is currently 28 years old. Even though his parents are well-known in the entertainment business, he opts to stay away from the spotlight. 

August's father, Michael, has played in numerous movies and TV series. He is well known for his role in the TV series "NCIS." August's mother, Amelia on the other hand is also a well-known actress in the film industry.

August was born during the marriage of his parents, Michael and Amelia. Their marriage was short and only lasted for two years. August was born a year later after his parents tied the knot.

A Brief Marriage Life: Michael and Amelia

Michael and Amelia are renowned actors and were popular actors during the 1990s. The two first crossed paths on the set of the soap opera "Loving." the two portrayed on-screen love interests, and, over time, their romance spilled into real life, leading to their marriage. 

The couple married after the show ended in 1995. The two tied the knot on September 26, 1995. Not even a year after, the couple welcomed their eldest son, August, in 1996. Unfortunately, the relationship between Michael and Amelia took a turn for the worse. Their marriage faced challenges during their two years together.

In 1997, after being together for two years, their marriage ended with a divorce. But, for their son August, Michael and Amelia decided to be friends.

Divorce of Michael and His Ex-Wife Amelia

Michael and Amelia chose to part ways and end their marriage in 1997, similar to Maceo Martinez's parents' divorce. Michael lost his job during his marriage with Amelia, which was a major cause of their divorce in 1997. He talked to People magazine about the challenges he went through in his marriage. 

After their divorce, Michael and Amelia stayed connected for the sake of their son, August. Michael married Dr. Bojana Jankovic in 2009, and they have two children, Olive and Liam. On the other hand, Amelia tied the knot with soap actor Thad Luckinbill in 2008, and they share a son named Rowe and Georgia.

Siblings of August Manning Weatherly

Although August is the only child of actor Michael and Amelia, he has four half-siblings from his parents' remarriages. Despite his actor parents showcasing his half-siblings, August chose to stay away from media attention.

August's father married Dr. Bojana Jankovic, and they share two children, Olive Weatherly and Lian Weatherly. His mother, Amelia, remarried Thad Luckinbill and had two children named Thaddeus Rowe Luckinbill Jr and Georgia March Luckinbill.

August Resembles his Father, Michael

Michael occasionally shares pictures of his two youngest children, Olivia, ten, and Liam. In contrast, August opts to stay out of the spotlight. Despite this, his mom has shared a glimpse of August on her Instagram, revealing his striking resemblance to his famous father.

After Amelia posted a picture of August on her Instagram, many commented on his resemblance with his father, Michael. One replied, "He's gorgeous like dad Michael Weatherly." A second added, "He is the spitting image of his father, very handsome."
