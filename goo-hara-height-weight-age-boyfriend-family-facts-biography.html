Goo Hara was a South Korean singer and actor who was a member of the South Korean girl group Kara. She was also a part of shows and movies like My Mad Beauty Diary, City Hunter, Urakara, Galileo 2, Invincible Youth, and It’s Okay, That’s Love. Moreover, Goo Hara made her debut as a solo artist in July 2015 with the release of her extended play titled Alohara (Can You Feel It?).

Girlfriend / Spouse

Goo Hara had dated –

  • Yong Jun-Hyung (2011-2013) – Goo Hara started going out with singer-songwriter, Yong Jun-Hyung in the summer of 2011. Their relationship was made public in June 2011 and lasted less than two years as the rumors of their breakup started circulating in early 2013. In March 2013, their breakup was confirmed by her then representative agency, DSP Media in a public statement. The statement claimed that their busy schedule made it difficult for them to spend time together, which eventually led to them growing apart.
  • Choi Jong-Bum – She met hairdresser Choi Jong-Bum on the set of the beauty TV program My Mad Beauty Diary and the two eventually started dating. However, the relationship was a tumultuous one and a drunken Choi broke into Goo’s house at around 1 a.m. on September 13, 2018, while she was sleeping and an argument followed which ended up with the police arriving at Goo’s house after Choi reported her for assault. She claimed that the incident was two-sided and both sides posted images of their injuries on the internet to explain their side of the story. Goo underwent a medical examination and it was found that she was suffering from uterus and vagina hemorrhage, and was also diagnosed with “cervical sprain”, “facial contusions and sprain”, “lower leg contusions and sprain”, and “right forearm and additional sprains.” After the incident, she filed a lawsuit against Choi for threatening to release a s*x video filmed without her consent. The aftermath of the proceeding took a toll on Goo Hara and she attempted suicide in her apartment on May 26, 2019. Choi was acquitted of the charge of filming the explicit video without permission in August 2019 but was sentenced to 1 year and 6 months in prison.

    Best Known For

    • Being the former member of the popular South Korean girl group, Kara. During her time with the band, they released several successful albums such as Revolution, Full Bloom, and Super Girl.
    • Being cast in the role of Choi Da Hye in the South Korean drama TV series, City Hunter, which was based on the Manga series of the same name.

    First Album

    Her former music group, Kara’s first studio album after her arrival was Revolution, which was released in July 2009 and managed to reach the second spot on the South Korean music charts.

    As a solo artist, she released her first EP, Alohara (Can You Feel It?) in July 2015. It managed to peak at the 4th spot on the domestic charts.

    First TV Show

    In October 2009, Goo Hara made her first TV show appearance in the reality TV series, Invincible Youth.

    Personal Trainer

    Goo Hara relied on Pilates to keep herself fit and to keep her figure toned. Apart from Pilates, she loved to go horse riding, which she classified as her fun workout. Lastly, she also credited a host of breathing exercises for her tiny and toned waist.

    She complimented her workout routine by eating a healthy and balanced diet. She, in particular, was a fan of smoothies made from bananas, almonds, blueberries, walnuts, and milk for her beautiful glow.
