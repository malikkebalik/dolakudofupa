Have you watched the Disney teen TV series, Hannah Montana? If you have, you will know Oliver Oken in the show. But who is the actor who portrays Oliver? He is none other than Mitchel Musso.

Mitchel Musso is an American actor, singer and voice actor who is famous for his role in Hannah Montana. Mitchel is in a relationship with his girlfriend Haley Rome and also has a decent net worth. But how much? Read below to find out.

Who is Mitchel Musso?

Mitchel Musso was born as Mitchel Tate Musso on July 9, 1991, in Garland, Texas, U.S. Mitchel is the son of Samuel Musso and Katherine Moore. He also has two brothers, Mason Musso and Marc Musso.

Mitchel Musso began his professional acting career at the age of 8 years old. His debut movie was The Keyman, where he played the role of a Cub Scout in 2002. He has a total of 44 credits to his name as an actor.

Mitchel, however, is best known for his role as Oliver Oken in the Disney teen TV series Hannah Montana. He starred alongside Liam Hemsworth‘s wife Miley Cyrus and Emily Osment.

Musso has also worked in the film industry as a voice artist. He worked in various famous projects like Phineas and Ferb, Monster House, Avatar: The Last Airbender, etc.
