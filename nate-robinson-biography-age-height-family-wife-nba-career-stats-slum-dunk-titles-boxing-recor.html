Nate Robinson Brief Bio And Career

Nate Robinson, full name Nathaniel Cornelius Robinson is a former NBA player born on May 31, 1984 with his height at 5 feet 9 inches. He is currently married to Sheena Feliz.

The 38-year-old was born in Seattle and played for the University of Washington before getting picked by the Phoenix Suns in the 2005 draft.

Standing at 5-foot-9 the former point guard played for many teams. These include, the New York Knicks, Boston Celtics, Oklahoma City Thunder, Golden State Warriors, Chicago Bulls, Denver Nuggets, Los Angeles Clippers, and New Orleans Pelicans.
