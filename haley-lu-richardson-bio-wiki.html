Haley Lu Richardson Bio (Wiki)

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, September 18, 2024

Who is Haley Lu Richardson?

Haley Lu Richardson is an actress and dress designer.

Her major casts can be summarized in her roles as Krista in the Hollywood comedy The Edge of Seventeen (2016), as Claire Benoit in the psychological horror movie Split (2016), and as Casey in the Gotham Awards-nominated movie Columbus (2017). 

Apart from acting, Haley is also a dress designer and designs and creates clothing and accessories at Hooked by Haley Lu since 2009.

Haley Lu Richardson: Birth Facts, Family, Childhood

Haley Lu Richardson was born on March 7, 1995. She is from Phoenix, Arizona. Her father is Forest L. Richardson is a golf course architect and her mother works as a marketing and branding professional.

She is of Mixed(English, German, Scottish, Welsh, French-Canadian) ethnic background.

She performed in theatrical productions and dance competitions at an early age. Haley was the leading dancer at the Phoenix-based Cannedy Dance Company since 2001 at the age of six and continued to dance with the same company for 10 more years until 2011.

In which school did Haley Lu Richardson attend?

Haley Lu Richardson was at the Villa Montessori through Middle School. After her Villa Montessori was completed she attended  Arcadia High School to complete her graduation.

Haley Lu Richardson: Early Profession Life, Career

Haley Lu Richardson’s professional life started as Tess of the Ravenswood. She began to appear in the ABC family series in 2013.

Eventually, she appeared on the big screen with movies like Escape, The Last Survivors, The Edge of Seventeen, and others.

Her performance in the 2017 American drama film Columbus is regarded as one of her best performances so far.  Apart from her acting career, she is also a dress designer and has her own clothing line called the Hooked By Haley Lu.

She is the founder of the clothing line and designs, creates, and promotes her creation herself.

Lifetime Achievements and Awards

In 2017, Haley Lu Richardson was nominated for the Gotham Independent Film Award for Best Actress for her role as Casey in the American drama film Columbus.

Haley Lu Richardson: Salary, Net Worth
