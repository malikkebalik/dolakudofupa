TikTok Couple Demi and Tom Schweers Welcome A Rainbow Baby

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, July 17, 2024
Updated On December 1, 2023 | Published By: Gracie Hale | In Child , Pregnant

Source: People.com

  • Demi Schweers and Tom Schweers welcomed their first child, a baby girl via IVF
  • The happy news comes almost two years after they suffered tragic miscarriages
  • The rainbow baby was born on Monday, May 15, in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania

Demi Schweers and Tom Schweers are officially parents

Demi Schweers and Tom Schweers have expanded their family. The TikTok couple announced the birth of their first child a baby girl named Nora Jude Schweers after several miscarriages.

Nora was born on Monday, May 15, in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, weighing 7 lbs., 6 oz., and measuring 20.5 inches at birth. They welcomed a rainbow baby via IVF.

The new parent exclusively confirmed to PEOPLE expressing their excitement on the arrival of their bundle of joy. They told the outlet,

“Holding her fills us with the most unfathomable sense of love. Holding her is the culmination of everything we have waited so long for.”

They continued,

“She took away all the pain and replaced it with the most beautiful and gentle love you could ever imagine. We feel so blessed to have a happy and healthy baby after trying for so long.”

Nora arrived two weeks earlier than her due date. Demi and Tom explained,

“She came two weeks early, but that just means we got to meet her sooner! Of course, we are biased, but she is so perfect. We knew we were going to love her, but to look into her eyes and see a reflection not only of yourself but of your partner, is such a beautiful thing.”

Congratulation to Demi and Tom Schweers on their first child and best wishes for their parenthood journey!

Demi Schweers suffered two miscarriages earlier

The couple, who amassed over 2 million followers on social media, has always been open about their pregnancy journey.

In 2021, Demi Schweers experienced two miscarriages following her diagnosis of both multiple sclerosis (MS) and polycystic ovarian syndrome (PCOS).

During her first pregnancy, she lost the baby at around  6½ weeks. The second, on the other hand, was believed to be a chemical pregnancy.

The new mama, 29, shared her pregnancy experience mentioning it was “challenging” while suffering from diseases. She explained,

“Navigating pregnancy while also managing MS was definitely a challenge. Multiple Sclerosis is so unpredictable, and taking on any new venture can daunting, especially one as physically and emotionally draining as an IVF journey,”

She continued,

“My body felt so weak at times, so having a mental health outlet was necessary to continue moving forward. Thankfully my husband, Tom, helped from administering medications to being there to listen during the difficult times. And now, postpartum we are blessed to have lots of family and friends around us to navigate motherhood.”

Tom Schweers expresses his desire to do for his newborn princess

While speaking to PEOPLE, the 33-years old social media personality also talked about challenges during the pregnancy journey, revealing,

“It was a difficult road to get to this point, with two losses and a challenging IVF process. My role during this journey has always been to support my wife as much as possible. She was the one being poked with endless needles, taking medications, and dealing with all the changes that come with pregnancy. Demi has been incredibly strong throughout it all.”

Besides, he also expressed his happiness at the arrival of his little one. He expressed,

“I am in love with our little Nora! Demi and I have always wanted to be parents, so to finally have our baby girl here with us is a dream come true.”

“And now I’m so excited to be a GIRL DAD! I am looking forward to doing all the things… putting huge bows in her hair, going on hot girl walks, and wearing clothes to match her cute little outfits. We already have matching bathing suits for the hot summer days! Demi and I couldn’t be happier.”

The couple has been married for almost three years. They tied the knot in August 2020. They presently reside in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania.
