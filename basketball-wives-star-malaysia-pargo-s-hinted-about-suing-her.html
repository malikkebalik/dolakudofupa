Basketball Wives Star Malaysia Pargos Hinted About Suing Her

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, September 14, 2024

On Monday, June 27, 2022, another intense episode of Basketball Wives aired on VH1. The show documented the women’s awkward situations as they attempted to preserve their work-life balance and repair their connections.

The sitcom did, however, succeed in fitting a lot of theatrics into a one-hour episode, which provided for some interesting viewing.

This week, Malaysia said that she was thinking about taking her ex-husband Jannero Pargo to court over his neglect of their children and failure to provide child support. She explored the idea of leaving the decision to God and seeing where things go from there with her friend Lala while looking for homes in the city.

In recent episodes of Basketball Wives, Malaysia has been vocal about her ex-husband Jannero’s failure to provide child support and support in raising children. Three of the couple’s children have lived in Malaysia for a while. The actor felt it was time for a bigger house because they were living in a little property in Los Angeles.

Lala, a friend, joined Malaysia as she toured homes with a real estate agent. Malaysia responded that her ex-husband was not helping with the property when the other person asked if he was.

She then acknowledged that she was thinking about taking him to court to settle the child support issue, but first, she would pray about it and wait for God’s response. Supporters offered their opinions about the situation on social media.
