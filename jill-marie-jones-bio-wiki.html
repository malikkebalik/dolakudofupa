Who is Jill Marie Jones?

Jill Marie Jones is an American actress, former professional dancer, cheerleader, and producer.

She is best known for her role as Antoinette ‘Yoni’ Childs-Garrett in the series ‘ Girlfriends’ from 2000 to 2006.

Jill Marie Jones: Age(47), Parents, Siblings, Family

Jill Marie Jones was born on January 4, 1975, in Dallas, Texas, USA. She is currently 47 years old. She has not provided any information regarding her parents, siblings, and childhood.

Jill holds American citizenship but her ethnicity is unknown.

Education, School/College University

She attended Duncanville High School and then attended Texas Woman’s University.

Jill Marie Jones: Professional Life and Career

Jill Marie was a Dallas Cowboys Cheerleader for two years and then a Dallas Mavericks Dancer for one year.

After that, she toured with the United Service Organisation and the United States Department of Defense Japan, Korea, Israel, and Egypt.

She began a career as a model and later moved to Los Angeles in order to pursue her acting full-time. She debuted her acting career in the series ‘City Guys in 2000.

After that, she played the role of Toni Childs for six seasons of the series Girlfriends and left in May 2006.

Also, she appeared in the comedy films Universal Remote’ and ‘Redrum’ in 2007 and in the films like ‘The Perfect Holiday’, ‘Major Movie Star, etc.

In addition, her other appearance includes ‘Gillan in Georgia’ in 2010, ‘American Horror Story in 2013, ‘Ash vs Evil Dead’ in 2015, etc.  She also made her appearance in the Ne-Yo video for ‘She Got Her Own’ and in the video ‘Got Your Back.

Awards, Nominations

She won FilmOut Audience Award in the category of Best Supporting Actress for ‘Drool’ in 2010, was nominated for the Image Award in the category of Outstanding Actress in a Comedy Series for ‘Girlfriends’ in 2006, and was nominated for BET Comedy Award in the category Outstanding Supporting Actress in a Comedy Seris for ‘Girlfriends’ in 2004.
