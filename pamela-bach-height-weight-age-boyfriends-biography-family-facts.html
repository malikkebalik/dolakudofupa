Pamela Bach is an American actor, producer, and voice actor who has been a part of television shows such as Baywatch, Solid Gold, Otherworld, Knight Rider, T.J. Hooker, George Burns Comedy Week, The Fall Guy, Cheers, Superboy, The New Lassie, The Young and the Restless, Sonny Spoon, Throb, Sirens, Viper, Baywatch Nights, and Celebrity Big Brother 2011. Pamela has also been cast in films like Rumble Fish, Castle Rock, Appointment with Fear, More than Puppy Love, Nudity Required, and Route 66.

    Pamela Bach Facts

  • In 1985, she shifted to Los Angeles.
  • Alongside Douglas Rowe, Michele Little, Kerry Remsen, and Garrick Dowhen, Pamela Bach co-starred as Samantha in the 1985 horror film Appointment with Fear (also known as Deadly Presence).
  • Between 1994 and 1995, she was cast in the recurring role of Dr. Ellen Baskin in the Canadian-American crime drama series Sirens.
  • Pamela Bach appeared in the action drama television series Baywatch from 1991 to 2000.
  • On December 10, 2009, she was charged with DUI for causing a rear-end collision in the San Fernando Valley area.
  • She was a contestant on the 8th season of the British reality television series Celebrity Big Brother in 2011 and she became the 2nd celebrity to be evicted from the house, being evicted on Day 14.
