Bluefaces Mother Threatens Legal Action Over Her Grandchildren

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, September 25, 2024

Blueface and his mother have a rocky relationship that continues to unfold in the public eye as the latter has now declared that she is willing to go to court over her grandkids.

On Monday (November 6), Karlissa Saffold took to Instagram to make it clear that she is not backing off from being involved in her grandchildren’s lives and that she will do what it takes even if her son doesn’t comply.

“If they deny me, then we’ll take the next steps,” the 51-year-old said from inside a hot tub. “Whoever needs to see what this process is like, you will be able to figure out how to do it yourself ’cause I’ma share all of the process and the paperwork.”

She added: “I didn’t bend over backwards and raise my muthafuckin’ kids to be denied my bloodline. You go tell them muthafuckas that mama comin.’”
