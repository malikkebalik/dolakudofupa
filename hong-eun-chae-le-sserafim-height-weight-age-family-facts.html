Hong Eun-chae Facts

  • She was a part of the student council and the athletics club when she was in middle school.
  • She was inspired to be an idol after watching SEVENTEEN‘s performance in 2018.
  • Hong Eun-chae was Le Sserafim‘s 3rd member to be revealed.
  • She was a trainee under Source Music for over a year.
  • She previously auditioned for JYP Entertainment and Pledis Entertainment.
  • Her fellow Le Sserafim groupmates have been Sakura Miyawaki, Kim Chae-won, Huh Yun-jin, and Kazuha.
  • On May 2, 2022, Le Sserafim debuted with the release of their first extended play Fearless and it includes songs like Blue Flame, Fearless, The World Is My Oyster, Sour Grapes, and The Great Mermaid. The EP peaked at #1 on the Japanese Hot Albums (Billboard Japan) chart, at #2 on the South Korean Albums (Gaon) chart, and at #3 on the Japanese Albums (Oricon) chart. Also, it was certified as ‘Platinum’ in South Korea.
  • The group’s name “Le Sserafim” is an anagram of the phrase “I’m Fearless”. The group was originally a sextet but Kim Ga-ram left the group on July 20, 2022.
  • Le Sserafim released its 2nd extended play Antifragile on October 17, 2022, and consists of the songs Antifragile, No Celestial, The Hydra, Good Parts (When the Quality Is Bad but I Am), and Impurities. The EP peaked at #1 on the US World Albums (Billboard) chart, Japanese Hot Albums (Billboard Japan) chart, Japanese Combined Albums (Oricon) chart, and Japanese Albums (Oricon) chart; at #2 on the South Korean Albums (Circle) chart; and at #14 on the US Billboard 200 chart. It was certified as ‘3× Platinum’ in South Korea.
  • As a member of Le Sserafim, Hong Eun-chae has been a part of many other projects such as Le Sserafim – The World Is My Oyster, Day Off, Day Off 2 in Jeju, and Leniverse.
  • Le Sserafim has won several accolades including the Asia Artist Award, Golden Disc Award, Seoul Music Award, and Melon Music Award.

  • She enjoys watching performances and mukbang videos.
  • Hong Eun-chae has a habit of charging her phone when its battery is around 50%.
  • She likes singer Rothy.
  • Her laziness is one of her cons.
  • Her specialties include making people laugh, eating, lying down, and sleeping.
  • She enjoys an iced vanilla latte.
  • If she could be any animal, Hong Eun-chae would be a black cat.
  • She has credited singer, rapper, and dancer Jeon So-mi as her role model.
  • She likes spring, her bed, and souvenir photos with friends.
  • In February 2023, she was confirmed as the new female MC of Music Bank.
