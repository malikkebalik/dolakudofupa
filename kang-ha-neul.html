Biography

Kang Ha-neul was born in South Korea on February 21, 1990. Known for his roles in such South Korean television series as The Heirs, To the Beautiful You, and Monster, this popular actor also starred as In-su in the 2014 horror movie Mourning Grave. Also a musical theater actor, he performed in stage productions of Assassins, Thrill Me, and Spring Awakening. He co-starred with fellow singer and actor Choi Minho in the 2012 series To the Beautiful You. Early in his career, he played the role of Kim Jong-hwi in the KBS1 series Hometown Over the Hill.
