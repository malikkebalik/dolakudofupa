Why Tarek El Moussa's Fiance Heather Rae Young Won't Have to Do Anything for Christmas

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, August 24, 2024

Tarek El Moussa and his fiancée Heather Rae Young revealed their plans for their first Christmas together as an engaged couple, and it turns out Young would not have to do "anything" for Christmas.

Tarek El Moussa's fiancée Heather Rae Young might just be among one of the lucky women who would not have to lift a finger for Christmas preparations because capable hands surround her.

El Moussa and Young are currently enjoying their vacation in Cabo but would make it back to California for Christmas. Although the couple does not intend to throw a lavish holiday party or do anything loud, they are hosting some friends and family.

El Moussa and Heather Rae Young at the Give Easy event hosted by Ronald McDonald House Los Angeles at Avalon Hollywood on November 07, 2019 | Photo: Getty Images

Young, 33, would not worry about getting the details right for Christmas or planning the dinner or anything as it has been taken care of. The realtor revealed in an interview that El Moussa's father and his stepdad are amazingly great cooks.

The two have told her not to bother about doing anything because they have everything covered. The couple is preparing for a memorable Christmas with El Moussa's two kids that he shares with his ex-wife, Christina Anstead.

El Moussa revealed that the house has been decorated with festive lights and set in tone for Christmas. To spice things up, El Moussa and Young revealed that they had already outlined the gifts they intend to give to the kids.

Their fans will be waiting to see how these two lovebirds exchange their much-awaited vows.

Young revealed that El Moussa's son Brayden is obsessed with sharks and would be getting a bunch of shark gifts while his daughter Taylor asked for a guitar and guitar lessons.

The couple also intends to continue their family tradition of wearing matching Christmas pajamas and posing for cute family pictures in it. Young said these silly moments with the kids where they get to laugh as a family is important in a year like this.

Since meeting in 2019, El Moussa and Young have become an adorable sight to behold as a couple. The lovebirds enjoy professing love for each other publicly and waste no time to shower affections.

Earlier this year, the duo celebrated their one-year anniversary with cute social media messages that warmed the hearts of their fans. Young gushed over El Moussa in a tribute she wrote to him on her Instagram.

The 33-year-old said she could not wait to spend the rest of her life with him and revealed that their relationship has been successful because of their decision to stay committed to each other.

The couple reportedly moved in with each other earlier this year and are planning their wedding for 2021. El Moussa recently gave details of their wedding plans in an interview where he professed his love for Young.

The 39-year-old revealed that there is a possibility that they might tie the knot somewhere outside the country as they had already ruled out Catalina Island where they got engaged and marked their first anniversary together.

Although the couple said that they do not want a super big wedding, they do not intend to make it a quiet one. Their fans will be waiting to see how these two lovebirds exchange their much-awaited vows when they have their wedding next year.
