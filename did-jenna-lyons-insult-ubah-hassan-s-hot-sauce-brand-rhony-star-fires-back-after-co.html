African-inspired UBAH HOT line

UBAH HOT is not your average hot sauce. Drawing inspiration from Ubah’s African heritage, each flavor in the UBAH HOT line tells a story. Take “Ruby,” for example, a sweet and robust sauce infused with Fresno peppers. This flavor pays homage to Ubah’s two Virgo nieces and their vibrant personalities. Then there’s “Yellow Tanzanite,” a rare and special habanero pepper recipe that honors Ubah’s beloved mother and her teachings of finding the good in everyone. And let’s not forget “Emerald,” a tantalizing blend of Serrano peppers, cilantro, and garlic, inspired by a powerful quote from Marcus Aurelius. Each sauce in the UBAH HOT line is a testament to Ubah’s cultural heritage and her commitment to creating bold and unforgettable flavors.

Ubah’s passion for flavors and cooking

Beyond her success in the modeling industry, Ubah Hassan has always had a deep passion for flavors and cooking. In fact, if it weren’t for her modeling career, she would have pursued a path as a chef. This love for food and culinary exploration led her to create UBAH HOT, a brand that allows her to share her unique flavor combinations with the world. Ubah’s journey from a small apartment in New York City, where she lived with fellow aspiring models, to becoming a renowned entrepreneur in the food industry is a testament to her dedication and creativity. Through UBAH HOT, Ubah is able to bring excitement and a burst of flavor to people’s meals, elevating their dining experiences and leaving a lasting impression.
