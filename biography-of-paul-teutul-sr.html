Biography of Paul Teutul Sr.

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, July 28, 2024
Date of Birth:1949, May 1,
Years:71 years
Nation of birth:United States of America

Paul Teutul Sr., also known as Paul John Teutul is a founder of Orange County Choppers which is a company that makes custom bikes. He came into the spotlight after he launched his reality television series, American Chopper . Besides that, he also wrote two books on automobile manufacturing.

Early life and biography of Paul Teutul Sr.

Paul Teutul Sr. was born Paul John Teutul on May 1, 1949 in Yonkers, New York, USA under the star sign Taurus. People who fall under this sign define their traits as trustworthy, patient, devoted, and responsible.

During his early days, he sailed as a member of the United States Merchant Marines Vietnam War, although he never served in a branch of the United States military.

Paul Teutul Sr. Career

After Paul returned from the war, he started his own company called the Orange County Blacksmith Shop, a fabrication shop now wholly owned by his son Daniel Teutul. While he owned the company, his son Daniel Teutul served as manager.

Paul Teutul Sr. started building custom bikes for his pleasure, which was inspired by many custom bikes that appear on the street and in the movies.

In 1999, he left Orange County Ironworks and founded Orange County Choppers to build custom bikes. Paul offered the company to his son Paul Teutul Jr. and began producing custom bikes for sale.

In addition to building custom bikes, he also published his first book titled Orange County Choppers in 2006 with his son Paul Teutul Jr. After a few years, he republished his second book titled The Ride of His Life in 2009.

Paul Teutul Sr. has also produced and acted in a few movies and series and some of them are Wild Boars and My Name is Earl .

American Chopper is the documentary series produced by him from 2009-2010. In 2010, he made an appearance in his documentary with his son. He also appeared on another reality series. Orange County Choppers – Made in America 2015-2016 form.

Paul Teutul Sr. Net Worth

Paul Teutul Sr. has an estimated net worth of $15 million . He has collected a good amount of fortune from his profession. Paul is also very popular for his collection of vintage cars from 1940-1960, each worth around $79,000.

In addition, as a producer, of his documentary series. Paul Teutul made around $100k per episode.

In 2017, Paul put his luxury mansion in Montgomery, New York, up for sale, valued at $2.9 million. His house was built on a 38-acre property that includes a hot tub, pool house, gazebo, volleyball court and water features.

However, according to the Daily Mail, in March 2018, he filed for Chapter 13 bankruptcy in New York, as his Discovery Channel show is scheduled to return to television. He even said that he owes creditors over $1 million and is worth $1.8 million.

Paul Teutul Sr. Personal Life

Paul Teutul at the age of 69 (2019) is single at the moment, however he has been married twice. He first married Paula Teutul in 1969. The couple was blessed with four children, a daughter Christin Teutul and three sons Daniel Teutul, Michael Teutul, and Paul Teutul Jr.

His daughter Christin is a nurse and his three sons are involved in the same profession; a custom bike builder. He lived his happy life with his wife and his children until the couple divorced in 1995.

Talking about his second marriage, he married Beth Ann Santos in July 2007 . However, their marriage could not last long and she separated in 2012 and officially divorced in February 2015.
