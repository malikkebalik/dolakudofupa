Enrique Iglesias Lifestyle, Biography, Age, Height, Marriage, Contact &amp; Information

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, September 15, 2024

Enrique Iglesias , born on May 8, 1975 (age: 47)in Madrid, Spain, he is a singer-songwriter. He began his career in 1995 with the song “Si tú te vas”, Enrique Iglesias went on to success with the hits ” Bailamos “, ” Do You Know ” and ” Hero  “. In 1996, the singer won revelation of the year at the World Music Award. The following year he received a Grammy Award. Enrique Iglesias has over 60 million albums and over 40 million singles sold worldwide. In addition, he holds the record for twenty-seven tracks placed #1 on the American Spanish-language singles chart, the Billboard Hot Latin Songs.
