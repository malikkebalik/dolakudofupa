Andy Foss Maine Marine Patrol Officer dies at 55

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, August 29, 2024

In a heartfelt remembrance, we honor the life and career of Andy Foss, a retired Maine State Police trooper, who sadly passed away on October 18, 2023, following a valiant struggle against cancer. Mr. Foss, at the age of 55, leaves behind his beloved wife, two children, parents, siblings, and a wide circle of friends and colleagues. Andy Foss, a former Maine State Police trooper, faced the daunting challenge of a cancer diagnosis back in 2017. He underwent a series of treatments and surgeries, ultimately leading him to retire from the force in 2018, a decision fueled by his determination to focus on his health.

Throughout his courageous battle, the local community rallied around him, organizing a benefit supper and silent auction in October 2018 to raise crucial funds for his medical expenses. Unfortunately, despite his relentless and unwavering efforts, Andy Foss succumbed to cancer on October 18, 2023, surrounded by his loved ones at his home in Machiasport.

Andy Foss chose retirement as a means to channel all his energy into fighting his illness. His diagnosis had led to this pivotal decision as he sought to make his recovery a top priority. The outpouring of support from the community was remarkable, exemplified by the Benefit Supper and Silent Auction held in October 2018.

He fought cancer bravely until the end. In a statement titled “It’s with great sadness we announce the passing of Retired Maine “

News of Andy Foss’s passing was officially announced by the Washington County Sheriff’s Office in Maine. They highlighted his incredible courage in the face of cancer. In the wake of his passing, heartfelt tributes and condolences have flooded in from every corner, recognizing his bravery and unwavering dedication to his profession.

The loss of Andy Foss is profoundly felt within the ranks of the Maine State Police and the broader community he served with boundless passion. His resolute battle against cancer serves as a testament to his indomitable spirit and bravery. The legacy of Andy Foss will continue to serve as an inspiration, not only to those in law enforcement but to all who knew his story.
