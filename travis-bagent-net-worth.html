Travis Bagent Wiki Biography

Travis Bagent was born on the 10th March 1976, in Charles Town, West Virginia USA, and is probably best recognized for being a professional arm wrestler, considered to be one of the greatest left-handed arm wrestlers in the world; he is currently a member of the team from Erie, Pennsylvania. Travis Bagent is the son of Jerry Boyd, a former arm wrestler, and Linda Bagent. He hails from a very rural area, where his family had a bar called The Big Arm Bar & Grill. He was very active in athletics during high school, where he played baseball, earning a scholarship to a B2 local school. He played for the school team in the position of pitcher, however, he soon quit baseball, and at the age of 15 he began to train at arm wrestling under the influence of his father, who was an arm wrestler as well. In his family bar, Travis met a number of the greatest arm wrestlers, including Bob Lear, Ray Patton, Les Whims, etc.

Travis joined in competitive arm wrestling in the same year that he began training; at the beginning, he didn’t have any major success, however, it didn’t stop him and he started training in Crossfit. His career turned professional in 1997, and since then his career has only gone upwards, as well as his net worth.
