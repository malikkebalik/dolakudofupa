Can This Viral DIY Flaxseed Mask Give You A Natural Botox Effect?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, August 22, 2024

TikTok has undoubtedly become the go-to platform for discovering unconventional yet effective beauty hacks. The latest craze? Flaxseed gel, touted as a natural moisturiser and even dubbed ‘nature’s botox.’ TikTok users and beauty influencers are sharing their experiences with DIY flaxseed gel, claiming it delivers a youthful glow and the much-desired ‘Botox effect.’ Intrigued? Let’s explore the details of this trending beauty hack.

The Flaxseed Frenzy

Flaxseeds, already known for their nutritional benefits when added to smoothies, have found a new role in skincare. Packed with omega-3 fatty acids, antioxidants and anti-inflammatory properties, flaxseeds turned into a gel can offer some dual-action punch—moisturising the skin and promoting hair growth. But can it really deliver a natural Botox-like effect?

The Science Behind The ‘Botox’ Claims

While the fatty acids in flaxseed gel contribute to moisture retention thus providing a temporary tightening effect, expecting results comparable to Botox might be a stretch. Unlike Botox, which targets muscle movement, flaxseed gel’s benefits lie in its ability to nourish the skin and reduce fine lines through hydration. While it won’t freeze facial muscles, it could offer a refreshing and temporary rejuvenation.

Recipe for Flaxseed Gel

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup flax seeds
  • 2 ½ cups water

Method

  • Boil flax seeds and water.
  • Simmer until a thick gel forms.
  • Strain while warm and transfer to a glass jar.
  • Cool and refrigerate. Lasts approximately a month.

    • How to Use Flaxseed Gel Mask

    • Apply a thin layer of flaxseed gel to your face and neck.
    • Leave on for 10-20 minutes.
    • Rinse off with cold water.

    Benefits of Flaxseed Gel

    • Skin Tightening: Provides a temporary tightening effect.
    • Moisturising: Great for dry skin, thanks to the omega-3 fatty acids.
    • Anti-Ageing: Rich in antioxidants, which helps reduce wrinkles and fine lines.
    • Exfoliation: Acts as a natural exfoliant, clearing dead skin for a radiant glow.
    • Collagen Boost: Supports collagen production, promoting firm and youthful skin.

    Due to its multiple benefits, flaxseeds are the latest hero ingredients when it comes to skincare. Here’s some other DIY flaxseed mask recipes that can address different skin concerns.

    Flaxseed Masks For Various Concerns

    1. Flaxseed, Honey, Lemon: Controls Acne

    • Benefits: Honey’s antibacterial properties fight acne, flaxseeds exfoliate and lemon clarifies for a blemish-free complexion.

    2. Flaxseed, Rosewater, Blue Clay: Removes Dullness

    • Benefits: Flaxseed gel adds radiance, blue clay removes dullness and rosewater provides a soothing touch.

    3. Flaxseed, Yogurt, Facial Oil, Cinnamon: Reduces Pigmentation

    • Benefits: Flaxseeds and yogurt lighten pigmentation, cinnamon offers antiseptic properties and facial oil provides nourishment.

    4. Flaxseed, Lemon, Multani Mitti, Honey: Brightening

    • Benefits: Flaxseeds and lemon brighten the skin, honey acts as a humectant and multani mitti aids in oil absorption and cleansing.

    The Bottom Line

    The flaxseed gel trend on TikTok is more than just a fleeting fad; it’s a natural, ancient skincare and haircare ingredient popular in many cultures. While the ‘natural Botox’ claims might be a tad bit ambitious, the moisturising and anti-ageing benefits make it a worthwhile addition to your beauty routine. Try these DIY flaxseed face packs and witness the potential glow-up your skin has been craving—all from the humble flaxseed in your kitchen.
