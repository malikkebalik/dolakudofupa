Where is 'Next In Fashion' Season 2 winner Nigel Xavier? Designer ready to launch his own collection

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, August 17, 2024

LOS ANGELES, CALIFORNIA: Nigel Xavier bags $200K and now has the attention of the design world with the title 'Next in Fashion'. Nigel, known for creating designs out of upcycled and repurposed materials, competed against fellow designer contestants Bao Tranchi and Deontre Hancock in the 'Next in Fashion' Season 2 finale.

Nigel impressed the judges and hosts, Gigi Hadid and Tan France, with a fun collection, representing his brand, creating a cohesive collection to showcase on the runway in three days. Social media users were quick to send out congratulatory messages to Nigel after the thrilling finale.

Nigel will also launch his own stunning collection on Rent The Runaway.
