Information About Her Education

Regarding her training, Quinn has a broad background in both contemporary and ballet dance. She traveled to New York to teach dance after graduating from high school, and she also enrolled at Hunter College to pursue an undergraduate degree in international conflict resolution.

Quinn Lundberg Is A Business Owner

Quinn, the spouse of Zach Galifianakis, began her professional dancing career as a dance instructor and performer. Afterward, she relocated to New York, where she made friends with Melissa Leschuck.

The two were passionate about the same things, and in 2008 they founded Growing Voice. The business concentrated on helping local leaders implement the necessary community improvements. Up until 2014, she served as the company’s vice president and co-founder.

Quinn, in a similar vein, co-founded LOOM in 2014 and served as its Chief Impact Officer until September 2020. She has been employed as an Impact Consultant since 2020, based on information from her LinkedIn profile.
