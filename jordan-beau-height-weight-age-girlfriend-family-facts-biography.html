Jordan Beau is an American YouTuber famous for his self-titled YouTube channel to which he uploads several hysterical videos of challenges, pranks, vlogs, and tricks. He was a scooter pro who enjoyed freestyling before he got in to YouTube full-time. His entertaining traits have gathered him a huge fan following of more than 600k followers on Instagram and more than 1.5 million subscribers on YouTube.
